## 📁 Command Category: Pet

### 💾 `/pet`

**Description:** 🐾 All commands related to the pet system.

### 💻 Usage

`/pet adopt <name>`
`/pet editname <name>`
`/pet feed`
`/pet gacha`
`/pet info`
`/pet leaderboard`
`/pet play`
`/pet sell`
`/pet use`

### 🔧 Subcommands

**`/pet adopt <name>`**
> Adopt a random pet

**Options for this subcommand:**
- **`name*`**
  - **Description:** Pet name
  - **Type:** Text
**`/pet editname <name>`**
> Edit your pet name!

**Options for this subcommand:**
- **`name*`**
  - **Description:** New pet name
  - **Type:** Text
**`/pet feed`**
> Feed your pet!


**`/pet gacha`**
> Gacha your pet!


**`/pet info`**
> View your pet info!


**`/pet leaderboard`**
> View pet leaderboard!


**`/pet play`**
> Play with your pet!


**`/pet sell`**
> Sell your pet!


**`/pet use`**
> Use your pet and get a bonus!
